Burger Recipe

Homepage

Description

Above, you will see a beautifully constructed Flitz burger. The taste is like no other and it can be made easily in the comfort of your own apartment. Get ready to construct a quick and easy dinner for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bun
  • High Quality Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Condiments (especially ketchup)

Steps

  1. Take your ground beef and turn it into 8oz patties
  2. Set patties onto cast iron skillet or Foreman grill and cook 3 mins each side
  3. Once cooked, place patty on brioche bun
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and any condiments needed.
  5. Finally, enjoy! And pat yourself on the back!
