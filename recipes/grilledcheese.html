Grilled Cheese Recipe

Homepage

Description

A fun, tasty sandwich that everyone knows. It's simple yet just as good as any other sandwich. It's the grilled cheese and I am gonna show you how to make it.

Ingredients

  • White Bread (or any kind of slices of bread)
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • A pan to cook it on

Steps

  1. Place down 2 slices of bread
  2. Place slices of cheese onto bread
  3. Warm pan up to medium heat
  4. Butter ends of bread before placing onto pan
  5. Cook until crispy and brown on both sides, flip when one side is done
